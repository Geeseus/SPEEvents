{{title}} What types of events does the subreddit host?

Contests: Subreddit contests where the best submission wins. The winner is usually determined through a poll and receives a custom subreddit name (old Reddit) and user flair.

Weekly Themes: Weekly events where a set of topics or subreddit formats are provided. Submissions need to be related to at least one of these topics (no contest/winner).

Slapfights: Biweekly events where 2 or more teams battle for victory. Join a team and create content to propagate it. The team with the highest amount of points wins and is rewarded with a new corresponding user flair.

SPECember: Yearly event hosted in December with daily shitposting topics. Participate on each day to receive special rewards.

 How can I participate in a subreddit event? Press the corresponding "Submit Post" button on the "Current Events" page to create a new event post. Make sure that the right tag (usually "[WT]" for weekly themes, "[Contest]" for contests and "[Team]" for slapfights) is included in the post title so it can be recognized by the event bot. How do I earn points for myself and my (slapfight) team? If your post includes the correct event tag in its title and is at least 3 days old, the event bot is going to assign points to your post (and your team) automatically. Please note that you can only earn points if the post has been posted within the event's posting phase. Please contact the moderators if you didn't receive any points for your post, so they can be assigned manually. How many points do I get for my posts? The amount of points per post depends on the number of upvotes:
Upvotes Points
10+ 1
50+ 2
100+ 3
250+ 4
500+ 5
You can't get more than 5 points per post.
